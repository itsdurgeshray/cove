Frequently Asked Questions

Can I access the workspace on weekends?

Yes, Dedicated Desk and Private Office members have 24/7 access using our secure mobile app.

Are meeting rooms included?

All memberships come with a monthly credit allowance for booking our premium meeting rooms.

Can I bring a guest?

Absolutely. You can register guests via the member portal, and they can enjoy the lounge areas for up to 2 hours.

Is there a minimum commitment?

Our Hot Desk and Dedicated Desk plans are month-to-month. Private Offices require a 6-month minimum.
